Computer Science INDUSTRY: Computer and Peripheral Equipment Manufacturing: NAICS 3341


What aspects of planning are there within the industry/organization?

Planning in the Computer and Peripheral Equipment Manufacturing: NAICS 3341 industry is commonly used, such as designing the PCBs and casing of the device. Also commonly planned is the software to drive such a piece of hardware. Including in the casement, is colour, fonts, and ergonomics.
